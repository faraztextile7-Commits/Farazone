We offer a 30-day return policy on all full-priced items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Return shipping is free for all orders within the continental US.

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping takes 5-10 business days depending on the destination.
